About Me

I’m Taylor Shimbashi, and I was born in Lethbridge, Alberta, but grew up and graduated high school in Taber. After finishing high school, I started my studies in the accounting program at the University of Lethbridge’s Dillon School of Business. However, in December 2021, I paused my education to serve a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, where I spent two years focused on faith, service, and personal growth.

Some of my favorite activities include waterskiing, surfing, hydro-foiling, basketball, and playing the piano. I love the thrill of being on the water and the rush that comes with sports like basketball, which I played throughout high school. Whether I’m competing on the court or creating music at the piano, I find joy in challenging myself and staying active.

In June 2024, I married my amazing wife, Natalie, and together we’re both attending Brigham Young University. I’m a pre-business student with plans to apply to BYU’s School of Accountancy. Life is busy, but I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and working toward my goals with Natalie by my side.
